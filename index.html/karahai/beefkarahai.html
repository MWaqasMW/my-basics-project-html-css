Beef Karahai

Recipe

Beef Karahi is yet again another delicious Pakistani dish that you will find at any dining table. It is prepared with various Desi ingredients like tomatoes, yogurt, garam masala powder, salt, ginger, garlic, red chili, green chili, and coriander. You can prepare this delicious recipe of Beef Karahi Recipe at home by following the method as it is, in case you do not know how to make it. This Beef Karahi recipe can be accessed from any Pakistani restaurant as well.
